Understanding how work week configuration affects your journal organization.

How Work Week Settings Work

Your work week configuration determines how journal entries are organized into weekly directories:

  • Work Week Days: Entries created on work days are grouped into the current work week
  • Saturday Entries: Automatically assigned to the previous work week
  • Sunday Entries: Automatically assigned to the next work week
  • Existing Entries: Will remain in their current locations
